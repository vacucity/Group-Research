# Citation Style

1. Use bracketed numbers [1], [2] for inline citations
2. Multiple citations: [1,3,5] or [1-3]
3. Author-year format on request: "Smith et al. (2023)"
4. Every substantive claim should have a citation marker
5. Citations should be placed at the end of the sentence they support
6. Do not cite papers not in the input list
